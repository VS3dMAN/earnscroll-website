Delete Your Account

EarnScroll: Screen-Time Gym

You can delete your EarnScroll account and its associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

If you can still sign in, open EarnScroll and go to Settings → Delete Account, then confirm the prompt. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Option 2 — By email

If you can no longer access the app (for example, you have uninstalled it or lost access to your sign-in method), email privacy@earnscroll.com from the email address you used to sign up, with the subject line "Delete my account". We will action your request within 30 days and confirm by email once it is complete.

What gets deleted

We permanently remove your live account data — your email or phone number and sign-in identifiers, workout history (exercise type, rep counts, durations), earned-time balances, streaks, and app settings.

What we keep

For legal and fraud-prevention compliance, we retain a single non-identifying deletion audit record (a user id plus a deletion timestamp — no email, name, or personal content) for 30 days, after which it is also removed. Camera frames and foreground-app data are processed on-device only and are never stored on our servers, so there is nothing to delete there.

More information

For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
