Delete Your Account

EarnScroll: Screen-Time Gym · Developer: EarnScroll

You can delete your EarnScroll account and its associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

If you can still sign in, this is the fastest route:

  • Open EarnScroll and go to Settings → Delete Account.
  • Confirm the prompt. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Option 2 — By email

If you can no longer access the app (for example, you've uninstalled it or lost access to your sign-in method), email us and we'll handle it for you:

  • Email privacy@earnscroll.com from the email address you used to sign up (this lets us verify the request).
  • Use the subject line "Delete my account".
  • We will action your request within 30 days and confirm by email once it's complete.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove your live account data — your email/phone and sign-in identifiers, workout history (exercise type, rep counts, durations), earned-time balances, streaks, and app settings.

What we keep

For legal and fraud-prevention compliance, we retain a single non-identifying deletion audit record (a user ID plus a deletion timestamp — no email, name, or personal content) for 30 days, after which it is also removed. Camera frames and foreground-app data are processed on-device only and are never stored on our servers, so there is nothing to delete there.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
