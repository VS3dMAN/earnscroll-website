Questions? We've got answers.

EarnScroll uses Google's MoveNet model running entirely on your device. It tracks joint angles to count reps and detect form. It's accurate enough to count honest reps and ignore half-reps — but it's not a personal trainer. We're continuously improving detection thresholds.

No. Camera frames are processed by the on-device AI model and immediately discarded. Nothing is saved, uploaded, or shared. Ever.

You get 3 Emergency Access passes per day. Each grants 5 free minutes instantly. You're never truly locked out of your phone.

Any app installed on your device. You choose exactly which apps go behind the exercise wall. System apps and EarnScroll itself are excluded.

The app blocker is currently Android-only. iOS doesn't allow third-party apps to block other apps. On iOS, EarnScroll works as a workout tracker and time bank — the earn side works, but the block side requires Android.

Free users choose one exercise during setup. To switch or unlock all three, upgrade to Pro.

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is coming soon. For now, your data stays on your phone.
